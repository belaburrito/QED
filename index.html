About QED

Who We Are

At QED, for over 20 years, our passion is providing strategic advice to interactive entertainment clients through a global team of experienced professionals.
See Our Services

QED Services

A leading video game agency providing strategic advice and access to the video game marketplace of publishers and private capital.

01.

We Get Games Made

We work with talented development studios that have original IP, helping them protect and grow their ideas into some of the industry’s biggest games.

02.

We Find the Perfect Match

We know that choosing the right partner is about much more than bringing money to the table. It’s about getting your game into the hands of your target players. Our 360 degree analysis ensures we approach the right partners for your studio and your game.

03.

We Know the Market

We are always gathering information from our global network of publishers, developers, investors, and other key stakeholders. This is coupled with our own market research to equip our clients with the timely and accurate information they need to make decisions about their business and their games.

04.

We Are Connected to Funding

In addition to deep multi-year relationships with virtually all major publishers, we are directly connected with the investment community. We have helped several clients with acquisitions and can provide firsthand M&A advice and connect you to licensed advisors when the time is right.
